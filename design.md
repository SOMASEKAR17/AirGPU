# Brand Design & Color Palette Guideline: Compound

## 1. Brand Ethos
The brand identity is **"Modern Financial Sophistication."** It balances the reliability of traditional high-net-worth banking (represented by serif typography) with the efficiency and transparency of a modern technology platform (represented by clean UI and ample white space).

---

## 2. Color Palette

### Core Neutrals
* **Primary Black (#1A1A1A):** Used for high-impact headings and primary CTA buttons.
* **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** The foundational background color to enforce a "clean" and "open" aesthetic.
* **Slate Grey (#4A4A4A):** Primary color for body text and secondary labels to ensure high legibility without the harshness of pure black.

### Accent & Functional Colors
* **Champagne Gold (#FDF2D0):** Reserved for high-level announcements and "premium" notifications (e.g., the top-bar banner).
* **Success Green (#4CC38A):** Used strictly for financial growth indicators, upward trends, and positive data points.
* **Soft Lavender-Grey (#F5F5F7):** Used for dashboard backgrounds and subtle card containers to create depth without using borders.

---

## 3. Typography

### Logo / Brand Identity
* **Style:** High-contrast **Serif** (e.g., *Caslon*, *Modern No. 20*, or *Publico*).
* **Usage:** Logo only. Represents "Old World" stability and trust.

### Interface / Body
* **Style:** Geometric **Sans-Serif** (e.g., *Inter*, *Graphik*, or *SF Pro*).
* **Headings:** Large scale, tight tracking (letter-spacing), and medium weight.
* **Body Text:** Regular weight with generous line-height (1.5x - 1.6x) for maximum readability.

---

## 4. Visual Language & Imagery

### The "Ribbon" Motif
* **Description:** A translucent, 3D abstract shape (DNA/Ribbon style) used as a background element.
* **Purpose:** Suggests fluidity, growth, and a "high-tech" bespoke service.

### UI Mockups
* **Style:** "Skeuomorphic Minimalism." Dashboards should look realistic but simplified.
* **Characteristics:** Subtle drop shadows, rounded corners (8px–12px), and clean data visualizations (line charts with gradients).

---

## 5. Design Principles for Implementation

* **Negative Space:** Prioritize vertical "breathing room." Section margins should be substantial (120px–160px on desktop).
* **No Borders:** Define sections using color blocks (White vs. Light Grey) or soft shadows rather than hard lines.
* **Alignment:** Content should be strictly grid-aligned but can feature "floating" elements (like the ribbon) to break the rigidity.
* **CTA Strategy:** Use a single, high-contrast dark button to drive action, avoiding competing bright colors.
